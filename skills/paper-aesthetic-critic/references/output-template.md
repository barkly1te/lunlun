# Output Template

Use this template when the user asks for a direct quality verdict.

## Verdict
- One of: top-tier ready / strong but not yet top-tier / competent but generic / polished but shallow / not submission-ready

## Why It Feels Strong or Weak
- 1 short paragraph explaining the overall sense of quality.

## Top Issues
- 3-5 bullets, ordered by severity.

## What To Keep
- 2-4 bullets preserving the strongest elements.

## Highest-Leverage Revisions
- 3-5 bullets focused on the largest expected gain.

## Optional Rewrite
- Only include when a title, abstract, contribution list, figure story, or outline rewrite would materially help.
